About

The need of the world

With air pollution levels rising day by day, it is our duty to realise the issue and take appropriate actions. Data visualisation plays a very important role towards helping in making better decisions and looking at the effects of our actions. It helps in simplifying the complex data and present it in a format that can be understood by everyone. It helps in delecting the hotspots of air pollution where further actions can be taken. The project was done to help create awareness among people and visualize the data in an accessible manner. The data used in the project are dummy values used only for visualisation purposes.
